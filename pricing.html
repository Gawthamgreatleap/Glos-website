Questions

Pricing FAQ.

❓

Do all plans get every feature?

Yes. Every GLOS module — Reviews, Tasks, Business Reviews, Routines, Culture, Chat, Contracts and Dashboards — is included on both plans. You only ever pay for seats, never for features.

❓

What happens after my 6th user on Free?

You'll be prompted to move to Standard, priced per user — so you only ever pay for the seats you actually use.

❓

Is there a contract or lock-in?

No. Standard is billed per user, per month, and can be cancelled anytime from the admin panel.

❓

How does billing work?

Payments are processed securely through Razorpay, with a full payment ledger and reconciliation in the admin panel.

❓

Can we get a custom plan?

For larger teams or custom SLAs, book a demo and we'll tailor a plan to your organisation.

❓

Can I switch plans later?

Yes — upgrade from Free to Standard (or adjust seats) anytime from the admin panel, with prorated billing.
